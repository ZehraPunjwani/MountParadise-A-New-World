Admission

Mount Paradise is FREE to enter, so join now and experiance the parallel universe. However in order to really become part of the family you will need a ticket. The ticket Prices can be found out through the Ticket section of our website. Book Now!

Tickets

TICKETS must be bought at arrival or over the phone. Tickets bought over the Phone need to be printed out BEFORE hand. Indivduals without printed E-tickets will not be granted access until ticket is printed. There is £1 fee charge in order to print E-tickets at arrival.
