Acommadation

Mount Paradise is highly known for it's exceptional and High Quality Customer Service. We take pride in our expetional Standards of Accommadations which enable us to ensure that our Customers have time of their life when at our resort. Below you will find the various Acommadations we offer in order to cater for all our wide range of customer needs.

Tower Accomadation

Tallest Acommadation building in Mount Paradise that has one of the best views, proving the best experiance ever.

Castle Accomadation

A more Old Fashioned Accomadation that caters to everyones needs

Building Accomadation

The standard Acommadation in the far end of Mount Paradise.

Vintage Accomadation

Vintage style Acommadation for those who still love the old times. The accommadation provides a whole tour of Mount Paradise and also gives our customers the chance to dress up as their favourite past character or person from history. A photoshoot is provided.
